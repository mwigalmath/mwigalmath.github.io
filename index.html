About Me

I am currently a postdoctoral research associate mentored by József Balogh at University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign. My Phd advisor was Xingxing Yu. I am broadly interested in algorithms, combinatorics, and optimization. When I am not studying mathematics, I am probably studying Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu.
